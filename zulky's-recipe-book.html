Ice-Cream

  • Time: One Day to Freeze
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Cream 600 mL
Condensed Milk 600 mL
Vanilla Essence 1/2 Teaspoon
Milk Chocolate Chip 60 g

Step 1:
Combine cream, condensed milk and vanilla essence in a large mixing bowl. Use an electric beater on high speed to beat until cream thickens.

Step 2:
Using a spoon, stir through chocolate until well mixed through.

Step 3:
Pour into a dish suitable for freezing, leave in freezer overnight.

Mixed Berry And Doughnut Cobbler

  • Time: 55mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Frozen Mixed Berries 500 g
Red Apples 400 g
Brown Sugar 1 Cup
Hazelnut Meal 1/3 Cup
Ground Cinnamon 1 Teaspoon
Cinnamon Doughnuts 8

Step 1:
Preheat the oven to 180C/160C fan forced. Grease a 23cm (1.25L) shallow baking dish.

Step 2:
Combine berries, apple, sugar, hazelnut meal and cinnamon in a large bowl then spoon into prepared dish. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes or until apples have softened slightly. Level surface. Arrange doughnuts on top, cut-side down, slightly overlapping.

Step 3:
Bake for 20 minutes or until apple is tender. Remove from oven and set aside for 5 minutes. Serve drizzled with thickened cream.

Caramelised Apple Jar Cakes

  • Time: 1hr 10mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 125 g
Brown Sugar 75 g
Jazz Apples 8
Thickened Cream 600 mL
Mixed Spice 1 Teaspoon
Banana Bread 550 g
Mint sprigs As Much As You Want

Step 1:
Stir butter and sugar in a frying pan over high heat until sugar dissolves. Cook for 2 mins or until syrup reduces by about one-quarter. Add apple wedges. Toss to coat. Reduce heat to medium. Cook for 8 mins or until apple softens but holds its shape. Stir in 2 tablespoons of the cream. Bring to the boil. Set aside to cool. Reserve 2 tablespoons of the caramel.

Step 2:
Meanwhile, preheat oven to 120°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper. Arrange apple slices in a single layer on the lined trays. Bake for 1 hour or until crisp and dry.

Step 3:
Use an electric mixer to beat remaining cream and mixed spice in a bowl until firm peaks form.

Step 3:
Divide half the banana bread pieces among four 350ml serving jars. Top with half the caramel apple. Spoon over half the cream mixture. Repeat layering with the remaining banana bread pieces, caramel apple and cream mixture. Drizzle with reserved caramel. Top with mint sprigs and apple chips to serve.
